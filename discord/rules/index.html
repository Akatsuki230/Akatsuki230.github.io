Akatsuki's Discord Server Rules

Server Rules

Non-compliance may lead to warnings, mutes, or bans. Some rules apply to all servers with Akatsuki. Certain rules also apply to DMs. Report inappropriate behavior or content leaks to an admin.

Rules that are marked bold are more strictly handled than normal rules.

  1. No deadnaming. Applies to pronouns and names
  2. No racism/hate speech.
  3. No homophobia/transphobia.
  4. No old My Summer Car builds/asking for them
  5. No word filter bypass.
  6. No alts
  7. Respect moderator decisions
  8. No doxxing (sharing personal information of someone else without their consent)
  9. Keep the chat lighthearted
