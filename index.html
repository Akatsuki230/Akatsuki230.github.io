Welcome to Akatsuki's website!

Akatsuki is a skilled programmer with a passion for creating. They launched a YouTube channel in 2019, initially focused on the game My Summer Car, but now covering a variety of games.

In addition to programming, they express creativity through their YouTube channel, exploring different aspects of gaming. Despite diversifying content, they maintain a love for My Summer Car.

In 2023, Akatsuki began sharing engaging content on TikTok.

Starting in 2021, they made modifications to My Summer Car, including the popular Asphalt Roads update in 2023. This gained them recognition in the gaming community, leading to participation in NexusMods.

Aside from this, they manage a Discord bot called Fembot, utilized in 23 servers. The bot boasts various features, showcasing their extensive programming skills.

They're always open to new project ideas and enhancing existing ones.
