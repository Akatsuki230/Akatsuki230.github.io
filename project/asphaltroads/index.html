Asphalt Roads Mod - My Summer Car

This mod for My Summer Car swaps the dirt roads for asphalt ones. It not only alters the texture of the roads but also their feel. The asphalt roads provide more grip, but they remain relatively bumpy for balance.

The mod offers several settings. For example, it allows you to keep the dirt race track between the grandma's and the landfill untouched. This setting is enabled by default, along with maintaining the normal dirt roads. It also enables you to replace the pathways leading to the houses with asphalt.
