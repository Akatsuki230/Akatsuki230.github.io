FemboyBot

A Discord bot by Akatsuki.

The support Discord has active updates about the development of the bot, a chatting community and even you can be part of the development by suggesting new features!

This Discord bot, crafted with care by a dedicated individual, provides numerous features to enrich your server experience. Below is a simple explanation of what this bot offers:

  • Anti Raid: This bot vigilantly monitors your server. If it detects a flurry of activities unfolding simultaneously, it sends you an alert, helping to ward off any potential raids on your server.
  • Chat Revive: When your chat goes dormant for a stretch, this bot can spur the conversation back to life.
  • Chat Streaks: If users send messages on successive days, this bot applauds these 'streaks', adding a fun element to your server interactions.
  • Chat Summary: Each day, this bot can provide a tally of messages sent on your server, helping you keep track of daily communication.
  • Feedback Commands: This bot can gather valuable remarks and suggestions from your users, fostering a feedback-rich environment.
  • Leveling: This bot can establish a 'levels' system, rewarding active users, and promoting engagement.
  • Logging: The bot can maintain a comprehensive record of activities on your server, ensuring transparency.
  • Moderation: The bot comes equipped with 'ban', 'kick', and 'timeout' commands, aiding you in maintaining harmony on your server.
  • Polls: With this bot, you can set up polls. Your users can then participate in these polls, promoting interaction.
  • Reaction Roles: This bot enables users to choose their roles by reacting to a message, adding a layer of interactivity.
  • Settings: You can tweak the bot's settings to align with your server's requirements.
  • Welcoming: The bot can relay personalized welcome and goodbye messages to users, adding warmth to your server.
  • Blocked Users: If required, you can restrict certain users from using the bot. This is a feature under your exclusive control.
  • Premium: The bot operator can bestow special privileges to certain users. This exciting feature will be introduced soon.
