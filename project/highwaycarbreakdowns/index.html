Highway Car Breakdowns

This mod adds randomly breaking down cars on the highway. The car spawn rate is very rare though.

How to fix a car if you spot one on the highway:

  1. Locate a car broken down next to the road (very rare)
  2. The guy next to it will say hello
  3. Inspect the car damage by pointing at the car and holding F
  4. The problem will be inspected and found out
  5. You can head to get the resources or fix it on spot
  6. You will get a random payment afterward
