Old Ferndale

This mod returns the Ferndale to older versions of the game, restoring its classic features and aesthetics.

Current Features:

  • Old skin
  • Remove scoop
  • Old engine pitch
  • Remove linelock button from steering wheel
  • Better removal of mudflaps
  • Another set of wheels
  • Red interior and white steering wheel option
  • Blue and red skins
  • Fixed textures from the bottom
  • Wheels from Build 178
  • Ability to select skins
  • Option for old license plate
  • Old RPM meter
  • Ability to remove rear mudflaps
  • Ability to bring back old wheels
  • Ability to bring back old suspension

More features are planned for future updates, based on suggestions from the community.

Special Thanks to:

  • Amistech for all of the original models and assets
  • wojskoda for most of the suggestions and some of the assets
